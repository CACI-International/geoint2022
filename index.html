MAGE

A customizable situational awareness and mobile data collection platform 

MAGE is customizable to support teams with a variety of different missions and use cases.  When a data connection is available, MAGE provides real-time situational awareness of team members’ observations and locations.  While operating in a low-bandwidth or disconnected environment, team members can continue to collect data offline which MAGE automatically shares with the team when connectivity returns.

MapCache

An application that brings advanced GeoPackage mapping features to the field 

MapCache is an easy-to-use mobile and desktop application that brings advanced mapping features from NGA’s GeoPackage library to those in the field. MapCache utilizes the functionality of NGA’s GeoPackage by making it available on mobile devices in a way that is as easy as using the built-in maps on your smart phone. MapCache gives users the ability to view, download and share GeoPackages offline.

OpenStreetMap

An open-source, crowd-sourced mapping data available on all security domains 

OSM relies on a diverse community of mappers, cartographers, and analysts using portable GPS devices, remotely sensed images, and other sources of knowledge to compile up-to-date, mapping information. Data generated by OSM is its primary output, not maps. Providing this OSM data allows users to do more than just display map tiles; users can download files for use in products or tools of their choice.

Ronin

Use advanced, commercially developed, image-based analytics 

Ronin is an NGA developed capability, designed to allow users to run a variety of automated, imagery-based workflows, such as automated anomalous change detection, co-registration, and orthorectification. GEOINT Services designed Ronin to be a modular platform that allows NGA to quickly bring in, scale up, and reuse a variety of imagery-based algorithms, regardless of the source.

GEOINT Tradecraft Suite

GEOINT Tradecraft Suite (GTS) is a knowledge-sharing environment that enables rapid transference of GEOINT mission-related tradecraft and expertise - analytic tools, techniques, and methodologies. GTS currently consists of three components Tradecraft Hub, BRIGHTIDEA and GAMBIT.
